Library Services

Access to Abstracts and full text of E-Resources, Circulation Services, Display of New Arrivals, Document Scanning, Photocopy & Printout Services, CAS, (Current Awareness Services), Selective Dissemination of Information (SDI), Reference and Information Services, Internet Services.

We started "Educate-Library" in 2020

Library has huge collection of books, journal and E-journals which cover the disciplines of Management, Computer Science,Information Technology,Biotechnology, Humanities, Social Sciences, Law, Environmental Science, Engineering, Buddhist Studies and other related areas.



Library Objective

i). To advance and support teaching and learning activities of the university. ii). To support research activities of the university.
iii). To procure, process, organize, display, facilitates access to Scholarly communications.
